Lodging

Taniti offers various lodging options:

  • Inexpensive Hostel
  • Large Four-Star Resort
  • Small Family-Owned Hotels
  • Bed and Breakfasts

All lodging is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.

Transportation

Most visitors arrive by air or cruise ship.

  • Airport:
    • Small airport accommodating small jets and propeller planes
    • Expansion underway to accommodate larger jets
  • Ground Transportation:
    • Public buses in Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m.
    • Private buses serving the rest of the island
    • Taxis available in Taniti City
    • Rental cars from a local agency near the airport
    • Bike rentals available (helmets required by law)
