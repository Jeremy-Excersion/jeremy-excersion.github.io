About Us

Welcome to the island of Taniti, a tropical paradise located in the Pacific. It spans less than 500 square miles with enchanting views, diverse landscape - everything from sandy and rocky beaches to breathtaking rainforests. Home to a community of approximately 20,000 locals. The Taniti economy mostly consisted of fishing and agriculture, until recent years, when tourism to the island started becoming more popular.
